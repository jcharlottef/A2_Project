About the Project

This website was created as a final project for the Programming and Data for Policymakers course at Harvard Kennedy School. It represents the skills we learned over a two week Code4Policy module. For more information about the class, checkout its GitHub repository.


Meet the Team

Anissa Abdel-Jelil is a Master of Divinity candidate at Harvard Divinity School. She studies death in digital space, with a particular focus on ethical frameworks for regulating the political economy of digital remains.

Rasim Alam is a Master of Public Policy candidate at Harvard Kennedy School. He is interested in research at the nexus of technology, public policy, and human rights.

Jackie Faselt is a Master of Arts in Law and Diplomacy candidate at the Fletcher School at Tufts University. She is studying international security and technology policy with a focus on cyber and AI policy.
